Top 5 of my favorite games

Geometry Dash

Game based on reaction created in 2013, that is currently known for its difficulty and fanbase.

Minecraft

A singleplayer game with notes of multiplayer, in the genre of survival that is currently one of the most played games of all time

Roblox

A multiplayer game known for its fanbase and the huge variation of games available to the public eye.

Overwatch

A multiplayer competitive team game, where you can play with a relatively nice variety of characters and abilities

Apex Legends

A multiplayer game with a huge map, that has a similar plot to fortnite with more realistic characters
